Privacy policy — Sleep Soundly

Effective date: 2026-05-22
Last updated: 2026-05-22

This is the privacy policy for Sleep Soundly, an Android sleep-sound app published on Google Play by David Rediger ("we", "us", or "the developer").

Summary

The Sleep Soundly application has no analytics, no advertising identifiers, no crash-reporting service, and its own binary makes no outbound network connections. (Premium sound purchases are routed through Google Play Billing — see Premium sound purchases below.) Sleep Soundly itself does not collect, transmit, share, or sell any personal or sensitive user data.

Data we access or collect

We access or collect no personal or sensitive user data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, or any other identifier.
  • We do not access your location, contacts, calendar, camera, microphone, files, photos, SMS, or call logs.
  • We do not use any Advertising ID, ad-attribution SDK, or analytics SDK.
  • We do not maintain a server. The Sleep Soundly application binary does not declare the Android INTERNET permission and is incapable of making outbound network connections from within the app.
  • Crash reports collected automatically by Google Play (via the pre-installed Google Play system) are governed by Google's privacy policy, not this one. We do not receive personally identifying information from those reports.

Information stored on your device

The following is stored only in app-private storage on your device. It never leaves your device:

  • The set of sounds you have selected and your per-sound volume settings, so the app remembers your last mix between launches.
  • Your master volume.
  • Whether you have opted into "resume on reboot".
  • Which premium sounds you have purchased (used to skip a Play Billing round-trip on every launch).

You can delete all of this at any time by clearing the app's storage (Android Settings → Apps → Sleep Soundly → Storage → Clear storage) or by uninstalling the app.

Premium sound purchases

Premium sounds are one-time purchases through Google Play Billing. Payment information is collected by Google, not by Sleep Soundly, and is governed by Google's privacy policy. Sleep Soundly itself only stores the list of product IDs you have purchased, on-device.

Data sharing

We share no user data with any third party. We do not have any server, partner, vendor, or advertiser to share data with.

Links to third-party sites

The app contains links you can choose to open. These open in your default browser and are governed by the privacy policy of the destination:

  • The "OEM settings" link in the in-app settings and onboarding opens dontkillmyapp.com, which is an independent community resource not affiliated with us.
  • The "Privacy policy" link in the in-app settings opens this document.

When you tap one of these links, your browser may send standard request information (IP address, User-Agent) to the destination server. We have no control over and receive no copy of that information.

Security

Because Sleep Soundly does not transmit user data, there is no in-transit data to protect. Locally stored preferences use Android's standard app-private storage, which Android isolates from other apps by default.

Data retention and deletion

We retain no user data because we collect none. Local on-device preferences are retained until you clear the app's storage or uninstall the app, both of which immediately and permanently remove them.

Children's privacy

Sleep Soundly is intended for adults. It does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us using the contact details below and we will delete any such information (in practice, none can exist because we do not collect any).

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where the change is material, post a notice in the app's "What's new" section on Google Play before the new version takes effect.
